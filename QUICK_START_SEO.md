## ‚ö†Ô∏è Common Mistakes to Avoid

### ‚ùå Don't Do This:
1. **Keyword stuffing** - Using keywords too many times
2. **Duplicate content** - Same content on multiple pages
3. **Thin content** - Pages with < 300 words
4. **Broken links** - Links that lead to 404 pages
5. **Poor mobile experience** - Not mobile-friendly
6. **Slow loading** - Pages taking > 5 seconds
7. **No internal links** - Pages isolated from each other
8. **Missing alt text** - Images without descriptions
9. **Outdated content** - Not updating old pages
10. **Ignoring analytics** - Not tracking results

### ‚úÖ Do This Instead:
1. Use keywords naturally in context
2. Create unique content for each page
3. Write comprehensive, helpful content
4. Fix broken links immediately
5. Test on mobile devices
6. Optimize images and code
7. Link related pages together
8. Add descriptive alt text to all images
9. Update content regularly
10. Review analytics weekly
