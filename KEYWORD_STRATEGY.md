# Raid Atlas - Keyword Research & Strategy

## Executive Summary

This document outlines the keyword strategy for Raid Atlas, focusing on dominating search results for "quad ou buggy Morocco" and related adventure tourism keywords. The strategy targets both English and French search queries with emphasis on local Moroccan market and international tourists.

---

## 1. Primary Target Keyword

### "Quad ou Buggy Morocco" / "Quad ou Buggy Maroc"

**Keyword Metrics:**
- Search Volume: ~500-1000 searches/month (estimated)
- Difficulty: Medium-High
- Intent: Commercial (booking intent)
- Language: French primary, English secondary
- Geographic: Morocco, France, Belgium, Switzerland

**Why This Keyword:**
- High commercial intent (users actively looking to book)
- Specific to your service offering
- Lower competition than generic "Morocco tours"
- Strong local relevance
- Targets both English and French speakers

**Implementation Strategy:**
- Homepage H1: Include natural variation
- Meta description: Lead with this keyword
- Internal links: Use as anchor text
- Content: Create comparison content (quad vs buggy)
- FAQ: Answer "What's the difference between quad and buggy?"

## 4. Competitive Keyword Analysis

### Top Competitors
1. **Quad Morocco** (quadmorocco.com)
   - Ranking for: "quad Morocco", "quad tour"
   - Strength: Established domain
   - Weakness: Limited content

2. **Desert Tours Morocco** (deserttours.ma)
   - Ranking for: "desert tour", "Morocco tour"
   - Strength: Multiple tour types
   - Weakness: Generic positioning

3. **Marrakech Adventures** (marrakechadventures.com)
   - Ranking for: "Marrakech adventure", "Morocco activities"
   - Strength: Location focus
   - Weakness: Not specialized in quad/buggy

- **Content Gap**: No comprehensive guides on quad vs buggy

- **Local Gap**: Limited local SEO optimization
